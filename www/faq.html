  • Ordering
    • Will tax be charged to my order? If so, how much?
      • We do collect 6% sales tax on orders that are shipped to a destination within the state of Florida, USA.
    • Where can I purchase prints?
      • Prints can be purchased online, via our PayPal shopping cart, or in person at The Surf Station and/or The Girl Next Door Surf Shop. For more information about the shops, please see the contact page.
    • What is The Surf Station? What is The Girl Next Door Surf Shop?
      • The Surf Station is the surf shop that Tory started in 1984. The Surf Station is one of the most well-known surf shops on the east coast of the United States and is dedicated to delighting its customers. The Girl Next Door Surf Shop is the surf shop dedicated to serving the needs of female surfers, and is right next door to The Surf Station's original store. Tory Strange Photography fine art prints are available for in-person viewing and purchase at the shops.
  • Packing and Shipping

    • How will my order be shipped?
      • Your order will be packaged safely and securely and sent via FedEx or UPS. Please contact us if you have a special shipping request.
