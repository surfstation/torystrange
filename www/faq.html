  • Product Questions
    • Is there a text overlay, or other watermark, on the actual prints?
      • No. The actual prints do not have a text overlay or watermark. The text overlays are only present on some of the website's images in order to clearly display the title of the work.
    • Are the prints signed?
      • We offer unsigned, signed, and limited signed prints. Please see the detail page of the specific photograph for more information. If you order a signed print, or limited signed print, the print will be hand-signed by Tory Strange.
    • What is a limited signed print?
      • First, a limited signed print is a print that is hand-signed by Tory Strange. Second, a limited signed print is a print that is numbered as part of a limited set of that style and size of print that will be created. We will not make available both a signed, and limited signed, print of the same style and size. If a print is made available as a limited signed print, then only one of the limited signed set is available at that particular style and size. An individual photograph may have two or more distinct sets of limited signed prints, if the print is made available in different styles and/or sizes. For example, an individual photograph could have three sets of limited signed prints if it is made available as: 1) 13x19 Luster print, 2) 13x19 Metallic print, 3) and 24x36 Luster print.
    • Are the prints numbered? Are any of the prints limited edition?
      • Some prints are numbered as part of a set of limited signed prints. Prints that are not numbered are open edition. Please see the detail page of the specific photograph for more information.
    • Is a certificate of authenticity (COA) included with each print?
      • Yes.
    • Is there more information about the mounting and framing options that are offered for sale with the prints?
      • Please find details about the standard mounting and framing that is offered here.
    • What is a "Luster Print" or "Epson Premium Luster Print"?
      • Both names refer to the same thing. This type of photographic paper print has a true luster finish and traditional photographic feel. This type of print comes out clean and sharp, with vivid color and smooth tonal range.
    • What is a "Canvas Wrap", "Canvas Wrap Print", or "Epson Exhibition Canvas Wrap Print"?
      • All three names refer to the same thing. This type of photographic print is made on a 23 mil thick, acid free, lignin free elegantly textured cotton poly blend. All canvas prints include a protective UV spray coating.
    • Are prints available in other sizes? Are prints available on other paper or canvas? Are other mounting and/or framing options available?
      • Yes. Please contact us. We will work with you to get you what you want.
  • Ordering Questions
    • How long will it take to get my order?
      • We know that you really want your order quickly. We work diligently to get you your order as soon as possible. However, printing fine art photographs can be time consuming, and it it a process that is best not rushed. Your order will usually ship within 1 to 4 weeks of receiving payment for your order.
    • Will tax be charged to my order? If so, how much?
      • We do collect 6% sales tax on orders that are shipped to a destination within the state of Florida, USA. The checkout process will automatically calculate the sales tax due based on the shipping destination.
    • Where can prints be purchased?
      • Prints can be purchased online, via our PayPal shopping cart, or in person at The Surf Station and/or The Girl Next Door Surf Shop. For more information about the shops, including location and shop hours, please see the contact page.
    • What online payment methods do you accept?
      • We accept orders via our PayPal shopping cart. You can pay through PayPal with any available PayPal funding source. For example, you can finish the checkout process by using your credit card or your existing PayPal balance.
    • What is The Surf Station? What is The Girl Next Door Surf Shop?
      • The Surf Station is the surf shop that Tory started in 1984. The Surf Station is one of the most well-known surf shops on the east coast of the United States and is dedicated to delighting its customers. The Girl Next Door Surf Shop is the surf shop dedicated to serving the needs of female surfers, and is right next door to The Surf Station's original store. Tory Strange Photography fine art prints are available for in-person viewing and purchase at these shops.
    • When I purchase online, how will the transaction appear on my statement?
      • Depending on the payment source selected during checkout, the transaction will appear on your statement as "TORYSTRANGE" or "PAYPAL*TORYSTRANGE".
  • Packing and Shipping Questions
    • How much does packing/handling and shipping cost?
      • The checkout process will automatically calculate the packing/handling and shipping cost based on the shipping destination and other options that you have chosen for your order. The standard shipping cost is $20 per item in your order.
    • Can I avoid packing and shipping fees by picking up my order in the shop?
      • Yes! You may pickup your order for free at the shop. To do so, select "Store Pickup" as your desired shipping method during the checkout process.
    • How will my order be shipped?
      • Your order will be packaged safely and securely and sent via a shipping service such as FedEx or UPS. Please contact us if you have a special shipping request.
