    • Will there be a text overlay on the print that I receive?
      • No. The prints that are sent to customers will not have a text overlay. The text overlay is meant only to show the title of the photographs on the website.
  • Ordering
    • Will tax be charged to my order? If so, how much?
      • It depends. We will collect 6% sales tax on orders that are shipped to a destination within the state of Florida, USA.
    • Where can I purchase prints?
      • Prints may be purchased online, via our PayPal shopping cart, or in-store at The Surf Station. For more information about our brick and mortar shop, please see the Contact page.
    • What is The Surf Station and how is it related to Tory Strange Photography?
      • The Surf Station is the surf shop that Tory started in 1984. The Surf Station is one of the most well-known surf shops on the east coast of the United States and is dedicated to delighting its customers. Tory Strange Photography prints are available in the store.
