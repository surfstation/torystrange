Black Metal Frame
For this option, the print is mounted to foam core and then framed in a simple, light, and contemporary black metal frame. A mat can also optionally be applied before framing.
Canvas Wrap
For this option, the print is made on a 23 mil thick, acid free, lignin free elegantly textured cotton poly blend. All canvas prints include a protective UV spray coating. The canvas wrap print is supported by a custom made wood frame.
Unmounted Print
This is our most basic option. For this option, we put the print in a crystal clear sack along with an unmounted foam core backing. The foam core simply supports the print to keep it flat and safer during handling.
