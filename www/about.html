Tory Strange, President of The Surf Station Surf Company, has been doing dawn patrol surf reports for over 25 years. He photographs the sunrise and ocean from the nation's oldest city, St. Augustine, FL.

From his home in St. Augustine Beach, Tory can watch the changes in the ocean while they happen and can be seen photographing the waves most of 365 days per year.

This website displays some of the best of Tory's photography.
